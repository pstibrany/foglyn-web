Get Foglyn

There are several ways to install Foglyn into your application:

  • via Discovery Connector (available since Eclipse 3.5/Mylyn 3.2)
  • by installing from update site (see instructions):
    http://update.foglyn.com/stable
  • by extracting ZIP archive (foglyn-1.7.zip, 1.5 MB) into dropins directory.
